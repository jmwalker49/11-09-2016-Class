Jelly + Peanut Butter = Time

How to make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

  1. Gather Ingredients: Peanut Butter, Jelly & Bread
  2. Gather Utensils: Plate, Knife
  3. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  4. Spread jelly on one piece of bread
  5. Place the jellied bread on top of the peanut buttered bread
  6. Optional: Remove crush
  7. Place on plate
  8. Enjoy
